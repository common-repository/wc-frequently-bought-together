== Description ==

<h3>woocommerce frequently bought together</h3>

When a customer visits a product page, visiter will be provided option to buy theme together of the related products usually bought together is displayed.

On checkout page customer can add product to basket just by checking the checkbox.

This plugin is 100% compatible with [WPML] and Loco translate.

- Amazon like frequently baught togetcher section on single product pages
